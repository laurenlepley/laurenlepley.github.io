College Works



Freshman Year

This art was creating between the months of August 2023 and May 2024. As a freshman, I took courses in beginning drawing and watercolor art. I used mediums such as graphite pencils, charcoal, ink, as well as watercolor paint.


This painting is called "Vulture on Rocks" and was created using watercolor paints.



Sophomore Year

This is the most recent art I have created, as I am currently in the last semester of my sophmore year. This art was produced from August 2024 to May 2025.


This first few images are from a tryptich painting. Our class was assigned to paint the same image using different art styles from artists. The first painting follows Van Gogh's impasto painting style. I took inspirations from his self-portraits. The second painting takes a more realsitic route, where I didnt use a specific artist's style but rather used techniques such as underpainting and glazing. The third painting from this set is a pointillism style painitng, taking inspiration from Georges Seurat.



This painting was created from a constructed diorama. The image is based on an analogous color scheme of yellows, greens, and blues. The objects in this painting are ones that I already had lying around my room, minus the wooden block. The colored papers that make up the background are sheets of matte colors.
